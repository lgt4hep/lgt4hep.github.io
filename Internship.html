LGT4HEP - Internship at a DOE National Laboratory


This traineeship program has an important componenet: internship at a DOE national laboratory. The trainee is expected to apply what they learn at university to work with a mentor at a DOE national laboratory on frontier computational-physics projects.

The traineeship managers will reach out to potential mentors at national laboratories and match the research project with student's interests. This opportunity will extend the trainee's research experience and give them chance to get familiar with a future career in a national-lab environment.

This traineeship is sponsored by DOE HEP Computing traineeship.
